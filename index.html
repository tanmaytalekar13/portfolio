Sociosphere is a platform designed to connect service providers with users seeking high-quality services. Providers can register under specific categories, showcase their portfolios, and set their charges and experience. After admin verification, their services are displayed to users who can book appointments through a queue system for specific dates. Users can rate and review providers based on their experiences, helping others make informed decisions. The platform includes secure payment gateway integration, email validation, and authentication to ensure smooth and safe transactions. This project demonstrates my ability to build a user-friendly, scalable platform with essential features for both providers and users.
